Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

Making Christlike disciples through the pastors, churches, and ministries of the Chicago Central District.

Our Vision

Every church and pastor on the district valued and recognized for their unique gifts, as each one is essential in fulfilling the district’s mission of making Christlike disciples together. The Chicago Central District serves as a resource for each local congregation and pastor, fostering collaboration to support, equip, and facilitate their unique missional efforts. Through the practice of interdependence among our congregations, the district operates under a unified partnership toward the collective pursuit of the Great Commission.

Missional Priorities

Developing Leaders

We provide growth opportunities for all—laypersons, those discerning a call to ministry, and ordained leaders—empowering them to serve the church and the world for Christ.

Resourcing Pastors and Churches

Every pastor and church is unique. We support their mission through resources for growth, networking, development, and spiritual formation.

Planting Churches

We are dedicated to long-term, sustainable church planting, whether in new areas or through revitalizing existing congregations.
