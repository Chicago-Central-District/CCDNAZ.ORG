A Joy-Filled Start

Our 2025 District Assembly is in the books—and what a meaningful, joy-filled time it was! We began Friday night with a powerful celebration service featuring the Proclamation Gospel Choir, honored our retiring ministers, and recognized those newly licensed for ministry.

Spirit-Filled Reports & Response

Saturday morning began with the business of the Assembly, but it was far from routine—we heard inspiring reports that reminded us of God’s faithfulness. Daryl Cripe brought a powerful challenge, calling us to seek after the one…until… Altars were filled as people committed to Holy Spirit-led relationships.

Ordination & Vision

We ended our time together Saturday with the sacred service of ordination, celebrating six ordinands. It was a beautiful weekend of worship, vision, and unity as we continue moving forward together as a district.
