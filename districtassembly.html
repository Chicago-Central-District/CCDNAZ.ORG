Download the Chicago Central District App!

Setting up the app:

  • Once you have installed the app, open it and “Sign Up.” If you already have a user ID from last year, you may be able to log in instead of creating an account.
  • Select the language you prefer and the District (Chicago Central).
  • Once you login, select the menu (three lines) at the top right of the app.
  • Select “Edit Profile”
  • Your information should be listed there, though you may need to select your “Language” and “District” (Chicago Central) again.
  • Click “Update User” and close out the screen and you’re all set!
