EC Camp

“Everyone Cares Camp” is a camp specifically designed for people with intellectual and developmental disabilities. Located in north central Illinois, EC Camp is attended by people throughout the Illinois / Indiana region. We strive to provide an overnight camp with many activities that allow campers to express themselves in a safe and fun setting. There is also a spiritual emphasis with chapel services throughout the week.
