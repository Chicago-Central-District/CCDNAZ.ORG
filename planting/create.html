Planting: Create

Church Planting Stages

Imagine Connect Create Build Launch

Going through the “connecting” process will prepare a church planter to CREATE an action plan.
He/she will receive a guide to walk through the creation process.
The action plan includes the following:

C

Church Details

  • Name, Location, Times
  • Style, Model, Support
  • Format of Services
R

Research Demographics

  • Rural, Suburban, Urban
  • Economic, Racial, Education, Age, etc.
  • Targeted People Group
E

Envision Church Culture

  • Vision Statement
  • Culture Statement
  • Culture of Evangelism
  • Culture of Leadership
A

Assemble Systems

  • Guest Follow-Up
  • Volunteer Recruitment & Training
  • Leadership Development
  • Giving and Finances
  • Evangelism and Outreach
T

Team Building

  • Recruiting
  • Vision Casting
  • Training
E

Engage Community

  • Social Media
  • Face-to-Face Interactions
  • Events
