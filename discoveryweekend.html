What is Discovery Weekend?

The Board of Ministry Discovery Weekend is designed to be a time of encouragement and support, bringing together candidates who are beginning their ministerial journey. This weekend focuses on spiritual formation, understanding and embracing Godâ€™s call to ministry, identifying and developing essential ministerial skills, and exploring the expectations for those called to serve in the Church of the Nazarene.

What is the process?

The preparation for the weekend will consist of the following:

  • Completion of the registration application
  • Receiving a local church board recommendation
  • Participating in two assessments
  • Filling out a spiritual background inventory
  • Completion of a confidential information questionnaire
  • Authorization for a background check (completed through Verified First)

All ministers seeking a first-time district license in the Church of the Nazarene must complete the Discovery Weekend before they will qualify for a district license on the Chicago Central District. For those currently licensed, completion of the Discovery Weekend is required prior to ordination.
