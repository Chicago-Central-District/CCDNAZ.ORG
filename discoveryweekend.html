A weekend of assessment, evaluation, and training designed to lead individuals interested in credentialed ministry in the Church of the Nazarene through a journey of self-discovery. This is a time of encouragement and spiritual formation for those beginning their ministerial journey. Participants explore God’s call, develop ministry skills, and understand expectations for ministers in the Church of the Nazarene.

Registration Information

  • Complete the Registration Application
  • Obtain a local church Board Recommendation
  • Participate in two assessments
  • Fill out a Spiritual Background Inventory
  • Submit a Confidential Information Questionaire
  • Authorize a background check (Verified First)

Costs & Registration

Registration includes lodging, meals, and materials.

  • $350 per couple
  • $215 per single (+$60 for single occupancy)
  • –$15 if StrengthsFinder was already taken
  • +15 if a spouse wants to take StrengthsFinder

At least half the cost should be covered by your local church.

Deadline: August 31, 2025
