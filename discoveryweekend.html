Discovery Weekend is a time of self-assessment, training, and spiritual formation designed to guide those exploring credentialed ministry in the Church of the Nazarene through a journey of self-discovery. This two-day event offers encouragement and support as participants reflect on God’s call, begin developing key ministry skills, and grow in their understanding of what it means to serve as a minister in the Church of the Nazarene.
