Discovery Weekend

November 7–8, 2025 · Lombard Church of the Nazarene

A weekend of assessment, evaluation, and training designed to lead individuals interested in credentialed ministry in the Church of the Nazarene through a journey of self-discovery.

What is Discovery Weekend?

This is a time of encouragement and spiritual formation for those beginning their ministerial journey. Participants explore God’s call, develop ministry skills, and understand expectations for ministers in the Church of the Nazarene.

Registration Information

  • Complete the registration application
  • Obtain a local church board recommendation
  • Participate in two assessments
  • Fill out a spiritual background inventory
  • Submit a confidential questionnaire
  • Authorize a background check (Verified First)
