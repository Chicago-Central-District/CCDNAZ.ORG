Licensing & Ministerial Credentials

The Chicago Central District is committed to supporting and developing leaders who are called to ministry. Whether you are exploring ministry, seeking a local or district license, or pursuing ordination, the process is grounded in clarity, accountability, and spiritual formation.

Our licensing journey includes mentoring, educational development, and collaborative oversight with local churches and district leadership. The path is designed to help you grow in theological understanding, pastoral effectiveness, and personal holiness as you prepare for a life of ministry.
